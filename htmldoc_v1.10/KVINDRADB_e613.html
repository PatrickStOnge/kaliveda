void ReadChIoPressures()
Read ChIo pressures for different run ranges and enter into database.
Format of file is:

RunRange  6001 6018
2_3 50.0
4_5 50.0
6_7 50.0
8_12   30.0
13_17  30.0

Pressures (of C3F8) are given in mbar).
