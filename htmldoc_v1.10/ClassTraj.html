class ClassTraj: public KVRungeKutta

ClassTraj

Classical trajectory for heavy ion collisions

Friction parameter ================== alpha=240 gives maximum fusion X-section for 40Ca+60Ni@6MeV/u (bfus<=5.25fm: 866mb) alpha=750 gives 66mb of fusion for 84Kr(605 MeV)+238U - measured fusion-fission Xsec from PRL 33, 502 (1974): 55+/-15 mb
